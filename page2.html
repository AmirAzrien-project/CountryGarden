INFORMATION AND DETAILS | COUNTRY GARDEN

Introduction

Country Garden persisting on it's core vision -"We hope the society will become better with our existence" and "With a constant ideal to be a conscientious, responsible, sunshine company" and integrated it's enterprise to a more comprehensive group including five-star hotel, education, commercial, and entertainment.

Planning and Design

Danga Bay in Johor Bahru is earmarked for the development of an International Finance & Commerce Centre. It will serve as a dedicated hub for financial players serving multi-national corporations and local companies that have established offices within the Iskandar Malaysia economic growth corridor.

Concept

Close to Education, Shopping and Leisure Hubs. 5-Stars Service Apartments By The Coast. 5-Star Lifestyle Amenities. The First Seafront Garden Oasis in Johor Bahru. 5-star Private Living Sanctuary. Managed by first class property management team that specializes in property maintenance, leasing and sale, tenancy and occupancy.

An exuberant lifestyle ✨
